Welcome to Oughtibridge Chapel

We're a family church located in the middle of Oughtibridge village and have a full programme through the week for all ages.

Whether you're a Christian looking for a local church or if you're interested in finding out who Jesus is and what the Christian faith is all about, we've got something for you and we'd love to see you or hear from you.

Life Explored Every Monday

What's the best gift God could give you?

We all want to be happy. Why is lasting happiness so hard to find?

Life Explored is for anyone who wants to find contentment and happiness in life. It's an informal and relaxed seven-week course. It's run by ordinary local Christian people and it's completely free. You don't need to know anything about the Bible, and you won't be asked to pray or sing! You can ask any questions you like, or you can just sit and listen.

Whoever you are, whatever you're thinking, Life Explored is a place for you to discover the greatest gift in the universe.

The Forum

The Forum is a group for men in the Oughtibridge, Worrall and Wharncliffe Side areas of Sheffield who want the opportunity to get out and do something new! It aims to provide activities for men during the daytime, including work projects in the local community, walks and talks.

The group generally meet at Oughtibridge Chapel at 10.00am on alternate Tuesdays.

We run a similar group for women, Ladies of Leisure. It is a group for women to meet and socialise together doing a variety of different activities. Please contact Brenda Birks for more details 07479946112 or brenbirks@hotmail.co.uk

Open The Book

Once every half term we do an Open The Book session at Oughtibridge Primary School. Open The Book aims to enable every child to hear the story of the Bible at school in their primary years.

http://www.biblesociety.org.uk/about-bible-society/our-work/open-the-book/

Discoverers

Discoverers is a fun lunchtime club for KS1 children (Y1 & Y2) held at Oughtibridge School. It is an opportunity for children to explore the bible in a child friendly environment.

Due to high numbers we run two groups, alternating each half term. The club runs Autumn and Winter terms but not in the Summer term, to allow the children to enjoy playing outside. Please Contact Us if you would like more information.
